Charles Lam's Homepage

I am a graduate student in the Linguistics Program at Purdue University. My research interests are syntax, semantics and their interface. Here's my CV.


Research Statement

My research interests are syntax, semantics and their interface. In particular, I am working on internal structure in the lexical domain (NP, VP and AdjP in the traditional sense). I am also interested in computational linguistics with corpus application.


Teaching

I am TA for LING 321 syntax and semantics. I was also TA for introductory linguistics.
